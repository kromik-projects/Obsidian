# French for Beginners - Course Overview

#french #course #beginner

---

> [!info] Welcome!
> This course is designed for **absolute beginners**. By the end, you will be able to hold basic conversations in French, express your feelings, order food, and impress your French-speaking girlfriend. Take it one lesson at a time, practice out loud, and have fun!

---

## How to Use This Course

1. **Go through the lessons in order** - each one builds on the previous
2. **Practice out loud** - French is a spoken language, reading silently is not enough
3. **Use the Notes folder** - write your own notes using the [[Practice Notes Template]]
4. **Review the Vocabulary Bank** - add new words as you learn them in [[Vocabulary Bank]]
5. **Come back and revisit** - repetition is the key to language learning
