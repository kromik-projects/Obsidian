## Talking About Your Job

### Asking About Work

| French | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| Qu'est-ce que tu fais dans la vie ? | What do you do for a living? |
| Tu travailles ou ? | Where do you work? |
| Tu fais quoi comme travail ? | What do you do for work? (casual) |
| Tu aimes ton travail ? | Do you like your job? |

### Common Jobs

| French (m) | French (f) | Meaning |
|-----------|-----------|---------|
| un etudiant | une etudiante | student |
| un professeur | une professeure | teacher |
| un ingenieur | une ingenieure | engineer |
| un medecin | une medecin | doctor |
| un infirmier | une infirmiere | nurse |
| un avocat | une avocate | lawyer |
| un comptable | une comptable | accountant |
| un informaticien | une informaticienne | IT specialist |
| un serveur | une serveuse | waiter/waitress |
| un cuisinier | une cuisiniere | cook/chef |
| un musicien | une musicienne | musician |
| un artiste | une artiste | artist |
| un vendeur | une vendeuse | salesperson |
| un homme/femme d'affaires | | businessman/woman |

### Talking About Your Work

- **Je suis ingenieur.** = I am an engineer. (no article before professions!)
- **Je travaille dans l'informatique.** = I work in IT.
- **Je travaille pour une entreprise de technologie.** = I work for a tech company.
- **Je suis au chomage.** = I am unemployed.
- **Je suis a la retraite.** = I am retired.
- **Je suis etudiant(e).** = I am a student.
- **J'etudie a l'universite.** = I study at the university.

> [!note] No Article Before Professions
> In English: "I am **an** engineer."
> In French: "Je suis ingenieur." (No un/une!)
> But with an adjective: "Je suis **un bon** ingenieur."

### Possessive Adjectives (My, Your, His...)

You need these to talk about family:

| | Masculine | Feminine | Plural |
|---|-----------|----------|--------|
| my | mon | ma | mes |
| your (tu) | ton | ta | tes |
| his/her/its | son | sa | ses |
| our | notre | notre | nos |
| your (vous) | votre | votre | vos |
| their | leur | leur | leurs |

> [!warning] Important Rule
> Before feminine nouns starting with a vowel, use **mon/ton/son** instead of ma/ta/sa:
> - **mon** amie (my friend - female) NOT "ma amie"

### Talking About Your Family

- **J'ai deux freres et une soeur.** = I have two brothers and one sister.
- **Mon frere s'appelle Tom.** = My brother's name is Tom.
- **Ma mere est professeure.** = My mother is a teacher.
- **Mes parents habitent a New York.** = My parents live in New York.
- **Je n'ai pas d'enfants.** = I don't have children.
- **Je suis fils/fille unique.** = I am an only child.
- **Ma copine est francaise.** = My girlfriend is French.

### Marital Status

| French | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| celibataire | single |
| en couple | in a relationship |
| fiance(e) | engaged |
| marie(e) | married |
| divorce(e) | divorced |

- **Je suis en couple.** = I'm in a relationship.
- **Je suis celibataire.** = I'm single.

## Hobbies and Free Time

### Asking About Hobbies

| French | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| Qu'est-ce que tu aimes faire ? | What do you like to do? |
| Tu as des hobbies ? | Do you have hobbies? |
| Qu'est-ce que tu fais pendant ton temps libre ? | What do you do in your free time? |

### Common Hobbies

| French | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| lire | to read |
| regarder des films / des series | to watch movies / TV shows |
| ecouter de la musique | to listen to music |
| jouer aux jeux video | to play video games |

| faire de la cuisine | to cook |
| voyager | to travel |

| sortir avec des amis | to go out with friends |
| danser | to dance |

| photographier / faire de la photo | to take photos |

### Talking About What You Like

| French | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| J'aime... | I like... |
| J'adore... | I love... |
| Je prefere... | I prefer... |
| J'aime bien... | I quite like... (softer than j'aime) |
| Je n'aime pas... | I don't like... |
| Je deteste... | I hate... |
| Ca m'interesse. | That interests me. |
| Ca ne m'interesse pas. | That doesn't interest me. |

Examples:
- **J'aime lire et regarder des films.** = I like reading and watching movies.
- **J'adore voyager.** = I love traveling.
- **Je fais du sport le week-end.** = I do sports on the weekend.
- **Le soir, j'aime cuisiner.** = In the evening, I like to cook.
- **Je joue de la guitare depuis trois ans.** = I've been playing guitar for three years.

### Sports

| French | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| le football | soccer |
| le basket | basketball |
| le tennis | tennis |

| la course a pied | running |
| le velo / le cyclisme | cycling |

**Jouer a** = to play (team/ball sports): **Je joue au football.**
**Faire de** = to do (activities/individual sports): **Je fais de la natation.**

## Daily Routine

### Common Daily Activities

| French | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| se reveiller | to wake up |
| se lever | to get up |
| se doucher | to shower |
| s'habiller | to get dressed |
| prendre le petit-dejeuner | to have breakfast |
| aller au travail | to go to work |
| travailler | to work |
| dejeuner | to have lunch |
| rentrer a la maison | to come home |
| faire les courses | to do the grocery shopping |

| diner | to have dinner |
| regarder la tele | to watch TV |
| se coucher | to go to bed |

### Describing Your Day

- **Je me reveille a sept heures.** = I wake up at 7 AM.
- **Je prends le petit-dejeuner a huit heures.** = I have breakfast at 8 AM.
- **Je vais au travail en metro.** = I go to work by metro.
- **Je travaille de neuf heures a dix-huit heures.** = I work from 9 AM to 6 PM.
- **Le soir, je fais du sport.** = In the evening, I exercise.
- **Je me couche vers onze heures.** = I go to bed around 11 PM.

## A Complete Self-Introduction

Here is a template you can customize:

> **Bonjour ! Je m'appelle [name]. J'ai [age] ans. Je viens de [country/city] et j'habite a [city]. Je suis [job]. J'aime [hobby 1], [hobby 2] et [hobby 3]. Ma copine est francaise, et j'apprends le francais pour pouvoir parler avec elle et sa famille. Je parle un peu francais mais j'apprends tous les jours !**

Translation:
> *Hello! My name is [name]. I am [age] years old. I come from [country/city] and I live in [city]. I am a [job]. I like [hobby 1], [hobby 2] and [hobby 3]. My girlfriend is French, and I'm learning French to be able to talk with her and her family. I speak a little French but I'm learning every day!*
